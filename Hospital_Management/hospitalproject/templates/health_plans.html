Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our health plans

What is included in the health plans?

Our health plans include doctor consultations, health checkups, medicine delivery, lab tests, and depending on the plan - video consultations, specialist care, emergency support, and health insurance coverage.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your health plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes will typically take effect from your next billing cycle.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All charges are clearly outlined when you select a plan. There are no hidden fees or surprise costs.

How does the consultation limit work?

For plans with a consultation limit, you get a specified number of doctor consultations per month/year. Once you reach this limit, subsequent consultations may be charged at a discounted rate or you can upgrade your plan.

What happens if I exceed my consultation limit?

If you exceed your consultation limit, you will typically have the option to pay for additional consultations at a reduced member rate, or you can choose to upgrade to a higher-tier plan with unlimited consultations.

Is there a waiting period for health insurance?

Depending on the health insurance provider and the specific policy, there might be a waiting period for certain pre-existing conditions or specific treatments. Details will be provided with your policy documents.

Can I add family members to my plan?

Currently, these are individual plans. We offer separate family plans that cover multiple members. Contact our support team to learn about family plan options.

How do i cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account dashboard or by contacting our support team. Cancellation will be effective at the end of your current billing cycle.

Are video consultations available 24/7?

Video consultations are available from 8 AM to 10 PM daily for Premium and Elite plans. Emergency support (Elite plan) is available 24/7.
